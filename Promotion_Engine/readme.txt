PROJECT:
Promotion Engine.

PROJECT DESCRIPTION:
This is a simple program that will calculate the total price of items (A,B,C,D) bought after applying discount (Coupon-A,Coupon-B,Coupon-C_D) on coupon selected by the User.

PREREQUISITES:
.Net Framework 4.8 and Visual Studio

EXAMPLE:
Unit Price of A: 50
Unit Price of B: 30
Unit Price of C: 20
Unit Price of D: 15

Coupon-A: 3 of A's for 130
Coupon-B: 2 of B's for 45
Coupon-C_D: C & D for 30
